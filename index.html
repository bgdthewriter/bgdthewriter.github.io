Travel to Seychelles

Seychelles is an archipelago country in the Indian Ocean. In 1971, with the opening of Seychelles International Airport, tourism became a significant industry, esentially dividing the economy into tourism and plantations.

Things to do in Seychelles

  • Chill on the Gorgeus Beaches of Seychelles
  • Hike to the Viewpoints for a Beautiful Panoramic View of the Island
  • Enjoy the Beautiful Sunsets in Seychelles
  • Go for a Scuba Diving in the Amazingly Clear Ocean Water
  • Feed the Aldabra Giant Tortoises of the Island
  • Enjoy the Nightlife in Seychelles

French and English are official languages along with Seychellois Creole, which is primarily based upon French. However, nowadays the language is often laced with English words and phrases. Including second-language speakers, Seychellois is the most-spoken official language in the Seychelles, followed by French, and lastly by English.[39] 87% of the population speaks Seychellois, 51% speaks French, and 38% speaks English.
